How to Play

🎮 Press Left / Right Arrows or A / D keys to steer.

⚡ Hold W / Up Arrow or Spacebar to BOOST.

🔫 Press F, E, Right-Click or tap SHOOT to fire bullets!

🎯 HEADSHOT: Hitting player head kills them instantly!

💥 BODY SHOT: Hitting player body severs and drops their tail mass!

🚫 Max 1 Bullet: Ammo capacity is capped at 1 (non-stackable).
